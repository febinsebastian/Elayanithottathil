History

The advent of St.Thomas, the apostle to Kerala in 52 AD is an accepted historical event. The successors of four families namely, Shankarapuri, Pakalomattam, Kally and Kalikavu who were baptized by St.Thomas settled in different places such as Kuravilangad, Pakalomattam and Kalikavu in the early centuries itself. Later a member of Shankara puri family (Njarakulam branch) migrated from Alangadu to Kurianadu and then to Nellithanathumala.One of his son named Mathen resettled in choodiya kavumkal property in Kudakkachira.

A sincere attempts is made to give a correct geneology of all the succeediary generation of the first members of Choodiyankal family. From the early 19th century. During this period, a few families like Vayanapra, Vallapra, Neriyara, Chekitananickal, Kumpalath, Kadumkunnel, Thottuchalil, who were related to Choodiyankal (the short form of Choodiya Kavumkal) lived in the nearby areas. A parish church is established in June 1887 under Muttuchira forane. Later Kudakkachira was put in Palai forane jurisdiction. The construction of church was completed in 1902. In the latter half of the 19th century the faithful had to go to Kuravilangad for the spiritual needs. The matters comiled from Kuravilangad and Muttuchira churches for the church register in Kudakkachira was useful for writing this history. The other sources of this family history are information collected orally form the elders and others book of family histories.

The elder son of Choodiyankal Mathen (he had four sons) names Ittiyavirah settled in the property of Elayanithottathil where there were a number of Anjilee trees. The history is narration of the descendents of Sri. Ittiyavirah Elayanithottathil (1834) Ittiyavirah had 3 sons as following


A. Mathai- Elayanithottathil (1864) Thekkilakkathil Branch.
B. Ulahannan- Elayanithottathil (1867) Kulippallythadam branch.
C. Ittiyavirah- Elayanithottathil (1870) Elayanithottathil.

Branch The history described in the website shows the decedents of these three branches from the sons of Ittiyavirah Elayanithottathil (1834).

Even these of 5th and 6th generation all of them still maintain their family name Elayanithottathil. They have migrated from Kudakkachira to Pala, Kajirappally, Erumeli, Ranni, Kollamula, Punchavayal, Chemperi, Chandanakkampara, Iritty, Alakkodu, Rayarom, Thirumeni, Cherupuzaha, Kadumeni, Chittarickal, palavayal, Chempanodu, Peruvannamoozhy, Mangalapuram, Bangalore, and Chennai. Some of them are also settled in Ireland, Australia, USA and Italy
